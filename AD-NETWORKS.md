## Recommended Strategy for Launch

### Phase 1 (Day 1 - First $100)
1. **Adsterra** - Primary network (Social Bar + Popunder)
2. **PopAds** - Secondary for instant cash (pop-under)

### Phase 2 (After 1K daily visits)
1. Keep Adsterra
2. Add **Monetag** interstitials
3. Try **HilltopAds** for video ads

### Phase 3 (After 10K daily visits)
1. Apply for **Google AdSense**
2. Apply for **Media.net**
3. Keep best-performing from Phase 1-2

## Ad Placement Strategy (6+ placements)
1. **Top banner** - 728x90 leaderboard (Adsterra)
2. **Sidebar** - 300x250 medium rectangle (Adsterra)
3. **In-feed** - Native ads between search results/posts (Adsterra native)
4. **Social Bar** - Sticky bottom bar (Adsterra Social Bar)
5. **Interstitial** - Before showing stories (Monetag)
6. **Pop-under** - On first click (PopAds)
7. **In-page push** - Push notification style (PropellerAds)
