### Tips for Choosing
1. Keep it **short** (under 12 chars)
2. Easy to **spell and say**
3. Include keywords: "gram", "insta", "view", "story"
4. Avoid hyphens
5. Check if social media handles are available too
6. .com is king for SEO, but .co and .io work too
