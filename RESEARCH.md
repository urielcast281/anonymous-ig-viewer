# Mollygram.com Research

## Overview
- **URL:** https://mollygram.com (redirects to /en3)
- **Full Name:** MollyGram - Anonymous Instagram Story Viewer
- **Tagline:** "Anonymous Instagram Story Viewer ❤️ Insta stories viewer | IG viewer"

## Layout & Design
- Clean, simple single-page layout
- Search bar prominently placed at top
- Content-heavy SEO text below the fold
- FAQ section with structured data (Schema.org FAQPage)
- Minimal navigation - focused on the core search functionality

## Features
1. **Anonymous Story Viewing** - View Instagram stories without logging in
2. **No Account Required** - No registration needed
3. **Completely Free** - No limits on viewing
4. **Multi-device** - Works on mobile, laptop, PC
5. **High Quality Images** - Claims high-quality streaming
6. **Publications & Highlights** - Not just stories, also regular posts and highlights

## How Their Search Works
- Simple input: Enter @username or paste Instagram URL
- Select the person from results
- View stories, publications, and highlights anonymously

## Pages
- Main page (search + SEO content + FAQ)
- Likely profile viewer pages (dynamic)
- Stories viewer pages (dynamic)

## Content Strategy
- Heavy SEO text explaining the service
- FAQ section targeting common search queries
- Keywords: "IG viewer", "Instagram anonymous", "watch Instagram stories"
- Hashtag-style keywords at bottom: #InstaStory #IGViewer #WatchInstagramStories #InstaAnonymous

## Ad Placements (observed)
- Likely display ads between content sections
- Pop-under ads common for this niche
- Interstitial ads before showing story content (common pattern)
- Sidebar ads on desktop

## Key Takeaways for Our Build
1. Keep homepage simple - big search bar, clear value proposition
2. Heavy SEO content on homepage for organic traffic
3. FAQ with Schema.org markup for rich snippets
4. Focus on "anonymous" and "no login" messaging
5. Mobile-first design is critical
6. Multiple content types (stories, posts, highlights, reels)
